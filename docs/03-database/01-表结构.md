## 表前缀含义

| 前缀 | 含义 | 示例 |
|------|------|------|
| `dict_` | 字典表 | dict_ports, dict_countries |
| `biz_` | 业务表 | biz_containers, biz_customers |
| `process_` | 流程表 | process_sea_freight, process_port_operations |
| `ext_` | 扩展表 | ext_status_events, ext_loading_records |

## 7层流转架构

```
备货单 (biz_replenishment_orders)
    ↓
货柜 (biz_containers)
    ↓
海运 (process_sea_freight)
    ↓
港口操作 (process_port_operations)
    ↓
拖卡运输 (process_trucking_transport)
    ↓
仓库操作 (process_warehouse_operations)
    ↓
还空箱 (process_empty_returns)
```
